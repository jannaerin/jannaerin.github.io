FAVORITE FOOD SPOTS

One of the best parts of traveling is trying new foods and restaurants, so I thought I'd share some of my favorites of the semester!

Dar Poeta


Home of the Nutella Calzone, Vicoletto Pizza (Pesto and Potatoes), and Superformaggi Pizza among many other favorites.

Da Remo


The best Roman style thin pizza I've had thus far.

Fatamorgana


One of my favorite gelaterias with a lot of unique flavors.

Fior Di Luna


Fred (a type of cookies and cream) is the best gelato flavor I've ever had!

Gelateria del Teatro

My favorite for more fruity flavors: Basil, Lemon, and Rosemary is my favorite and the Lemon is great too.
